Veterans Benefits Processing

Whether you're a veteran, an active service member, or a dependent, our team is here to ensure that your transition to SHSU is seamless and successful.

Created in 2023, our department provides personalized assistance as you navigate the process of utilizing your veteran and dependent education benefits.

What We Provide

  • Clear answers to your questions about VA and TVC education benefits.
  • Timely updates and support to ensure you meet deadlines and requirements.
  • All documents must be submitted through our online certification request system.

VA Educational Benefits

VA educational benefits are federal benefits earned by a service member or veteran. Benefit entitlement can change due to length of service, discharge characterization, and home of record/place of entry.

For more information, visit the VA website.

Hazlewood Act (Texas)

Veterans who entered the service with Texas as their home of record or place of entry may qualify for the Hazlewood Act, which provides 150 credit hours of higher education benefits.

More information is available at the Texas Veterans Commission website.
