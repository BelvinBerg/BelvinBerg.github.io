Use Your Benefits

Whether you're a veteran, current military member, or eligible dependent, SHSU offers multiple educational benefit options to support your pursuit of higher education. This section provides guidance on how to effectively utilize your benefits for maximum impact on your academic journey.

  • Determine your eligibility by reviewing available federal and state programs
  • Gather all required documentation and forms
  • Submit your application through the appropriate channels
  • Monitor your certification status and benefit payments
  • Contact our office if you need assistance at any step

Benefit Certification Information

Certification is the process that confirms your enrollment and eligibility for educational benefits. This ensures that your benefits are properly applied to your tuition, fees, and other educational expenses. Our team handles certification for both federal VA benefits and state Hazlewood waivers.

Federal Certification

For VA benefits like Post-9/11 GI Bill, we certify your enrollment directly with the VA. This includes verifying your course load, program of study, and benefit entitlement. Certification typically occurs at the beginning of each semester.

State Certification

Hazlewood waiver certifications are processed through SHSU's Financial Aid Office. We verify your eligibility for the Texas veteran education benefit and ensure proper application of the waiver to your tuition charges.
