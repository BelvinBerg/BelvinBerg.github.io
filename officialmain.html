Use Your Benefits

Whether you're a veteran, current military member, or eligible dependent, SHSU offers multiple educational benefit options to support your pursuit of higher education. This section provides guidance on how to effectively utilize your benefits for maximum impact on your academic journey.

  • Fully online document submission system.
  • Step-by-step guiadance on benefit utilization
  • Clear answers to your questions about VA and TVC education benefits.
  • Timely updates and support to ensure you meet deadlines and requirements.

All documents must be submitted through our online certification request system. Documents submitted via email, fax, or mail will not be accepted. ​

Benefit Certification Information

​​We are dedicated to honoring the service of our military-affiliated students by providing unwavering support, expert guidance, and access to the educational benefits they’ve earned.

Federal Benefits

​VA (Veterans Affairs) educational benefits are federal benefits earned by a service member or veteran. Benefit entitlement can change due to length of service, discharge characterization, as well as home of record/place of entry. For more information or to see if you qualify visit the VA website.

Hazlewood Benefits

Veterans that entered the service with residence or place of entry as Texas, the Lone Star State thanks you for your service with the Hazlewood Act. This Act provides veterans with 150 credit hours to utilize in higher education. Check out more information at the Texas Veterans Commission website.
